ABOUT US

Action Lanes was established in 2004 when four bowling enthusiasts came together and wanted to fill the void of a bowling center in their local community. Inside a 35,000+ square foot building, a fully-remodeled and furnished family entertainment center was born.Catering to the Greater Los Angeles area, we wanted to provide a safe environment for the whole family. These are the amenities that we provide for our customers:

  • 32 Synthetic Lanes with fun and uniquely animated scoreboards
  • Top-of-the-line sound and lighting equipment
  • Pro Shop conveniently located for the bowler’s needs
  • Snack Bar providing delicious refreshments
  • Arcade Games located throughout the building
  • Bar Lounge serving ice cold beers and wine
  • Rooms to host Birthday Parties, Private Parties, etc.

Action Lanes is the San Gabriel Valley’s premier bowling center, so we invite you to come see what we’re about!
